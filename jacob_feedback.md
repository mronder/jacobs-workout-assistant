# Workout App — Jacob's Feedback

---

## 🔴 Bugs / Broken Behavior

- Custom exercises don't persist if the app is closed before tapping "Finish Workout"
- Custom exercises don't carry forward to future weeks
- Push/Pull/Legs split only appears when selecting 3 days/week — should be available for 6 days/week

---

## 🟡 Core Feature Gaps
*(the app promises these but doesn't deliver)*

- Custom exercises should get AI-suggested rep/set ranges based on the user's goals (same as auto-selected exercises)
- Need a fully custom workout builder — user builds from scratch, app suggests rep/set ranges and weight progression
- Program continuity past Week 4 — does it loop, progress, or reset? Needs a defined answer and UX

---

## 🟢 High-Value Additions
*(real training features, not scope creep)*

- Progressive overload suggestions — recommend weight increases each session based on exercise and goal
- Deload weeks inserted automatically at optimal intervals
- Bodyweight tracking
- Expanded onboarding questions — surface mobility and stretching goals, not just lifting

---

## ⚪ Nice-to-Have / Separate App

- Nutrition tracker — macro targets based on weight goals, food log by text or barcode scan

---

## Priority Notes

Fix the **red items** first — they make the app feel broken. The **yellow items** complete the product. The **green items** make it good. The nutrition tracker is a separate app; don't touch it until the core is solid.
